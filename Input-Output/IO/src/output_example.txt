The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series
that depicts the everyday work lives of office employees at the
Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of the fictional Dunder Mifflin. Paper Company.
It aired on NBC from March 24, 2005, to May 16, 2013, spanning a total of nine seasons. [1]
Based on the 2001-2003 BBC series of the same name created by Ricky Gervais and Stephen Merchant,
it was adapted for American television by Greg Daniels, a veteran writer
for Saturday Night Live, King of the Hill, and The Simpsons.
